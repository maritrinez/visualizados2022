Hi, I'm Beatriz.

I am a Data Visualization designer and developer, with a background in social and market research.

After graduating as a Sociologist and a Publicist, I worked as a social and market researcher for five years. Of course I liked and I still like being a researcher, but what attracted me the most was finding revealing insights and communicating them in a clear and engaging way.

In 2012 I started teaching myself about the theory and practice of Data Visualization. At this point the code came into my life. I realized then that developing the visualizations was as fun as finding and communicating the research insights. I specially enjoyed working with D3.js, in which I specialized.

One year later I started my career as a Data Visualizer.

Currently, I work creating unique, made-to-order data visualizations, expressly designed for a specific dataset, so I mainly work with static data, no matter how big or small. I also create reusable data visualizations, for those datasets with identical structure but different content.

I love mails, hearing about new projects and challenges so Feel free to contact me with any questions, any data project or even just to say hello
